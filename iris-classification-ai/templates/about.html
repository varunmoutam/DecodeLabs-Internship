Input Feature Reference

🌿 Sepal Length
Length of the outer flower leaf (sepal) in centimetres. Range in dataset: 4.3–7.9 cm.
🌿 Sepal Width
Width of the sepal in centimetres. Setosa tends to have the widest sepals. Range: 2.0–4.4 cm.
🌸 Petal Length
Length of the inner flower leaf (petal). Highly discriminative feature. Range: 1.0–6.9 cm.
🌸 Petal Width
Width of the petal — along with petal length, the most important feature for classification. Range: 0.1–2.5 cm.
